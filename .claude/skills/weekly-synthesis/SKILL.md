## Task

Generate a Weekly Strategic Synthesis.

## Output Structure

### 1. Executive Strategic Pulse

The 3 most important insights from the current repository.

### 2. Known

Facts directly supported by the repository.

### 3. Inferred

Patterns or implications derived from multiple sources.

### 4. Uncertain

Gaps, assumptions, contradictions, or missing evidence.

### 5. Emerging Patterns

Patterns appearing across clients, decisions, research, trends, or signals.

### 6. Hypotheses Review

For each active or implied hypothesis:

- hypothesis
- evidence supporting it
- evidence contradicting it
- confidence level
- next evidence needed

### 7. Opportunities

Identify opportunities supported by at least two independent sources.

### 8. Risks to Monitor

Strategic risks emerging from the repository.

### 9. Recommended Memory Updates

Propose updates to:

- memory/strategic_memory.md
- memory/opportunities.md
- memory/strategic_hypotheses.md
- memory/future_signals.md

Do not modify files unless Natalia explicitly says so.

## Strategic Bias

Prefer insight over information.

Prefer synthesis over summarization.

Prefer implications over observations.

Prefer hypotheses over conclusions.
