## Key Signal

Experian has launched an Agent Operating System (Agent OS) specifically for financial services — a platform enabling trusted agentic AI in credit, fraud, and customer decisions. The accompanying global survey (April–May 2026, senior decision-makers at financial institutions) included Colombia and Chile among the surveyed countries, signaling that agentic AI adoption is being actively tracked in Banco Andino's home markets. The framing centers on "trusted" agentic AI — emphasizing governance and human-in-the-loop control as differentiators.

## Why It Matters

Agentic AI in financial services is moving from concept to product. When a major data and analytics platform launches a purpose-built agent OS for banks and explicitly surveys LatAm markets, the adoption curve is no longer theoretical. The "trusted" framing is strategically significant: it signals that the market is not asking "should we use AI agents?" but "how do we govern them?" This is a strategic maturity inflection that will reach LatAm banks within 12–24 months.
