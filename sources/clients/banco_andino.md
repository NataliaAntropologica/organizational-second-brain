# Banco Andino

## Industry
Banking

## Overview

Banco Andino is a regional financial institution operating in Colombia, Peru, and Chile.

## Strategic Challenges

- Long credit approval cycles
- Increasing competition from fintechs
- Fragmented customer data
- Low adoption of AI capabilities

## Current Initiatives

- Digital transformation
- AI-powered commercial assistant
- Customer intelligence platform

## Strategic Questions

- How can AI improve decision-making?
- Which capabilities should be built internally?
- How can customer trust be preserved?
