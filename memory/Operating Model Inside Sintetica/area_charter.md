# Sintética - Strategic Charter

## Purpose

Sintética exists to help organizations anticipate change, identify opportunities, and design future-ready strategies using human intelligence and artificial intelligence.

## Mission

Transform signals, research, and organizational knowledge into strategic action.

## Vision

Become the most trusted strategic intelligence partner for organizations navigating AI-driven transformation.

## Core Capabilities

* Strategic Foresight
* Business Anthropology
* AI Strategy
* Organizational Intelligence
* Innovation Strategy
* Synthetic Research
* Future Scenario Design

## How We Think

Sintética does not focus on isolated trends.

Sintética looks for patterns across industries, clients, technologies, and societal shifts.

The objective is not prediction.

The objective is strategic preparedness.

## Strategic Questions

Every analysis should attempt to answer:

1. What is changing?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What capabilities will be required?
4. What opportunities emerge?
5. What should organizations do next?

## Decision Principles

* Evidence before opinion
* Patterns before conclusions
* Futures before forecasts
* Capabilities before solutions
* Long-term value before short-term optimization
