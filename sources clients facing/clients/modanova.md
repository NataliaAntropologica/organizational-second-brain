# ModaNova

## Industry
Luxury Fashion Retail

## Overview

ModaNova is a premium fashion retailer operating across Latin America.

## Strategic Challenges

- Declining customer loyalty
- Increased competition from digital-first brands
- Limited personalization capabilities
- Fragmented customer experience

## Current Initiatives

- CRM modernization
- Customer data platform
- AI-powered product recommendations

## Strategic Questions

- How can AI create differentiated customer experiences?
- Which customer signals should be prioritized?
- How can loyalty become predictive rather than reactive?
